<div class="wordwrap">Dedicated to documenting the untold stories of severe mental illness, I am a clinical researcher and journalist investigating psychiatric hospitalization and alternative, non-hospital-based ways of recovery, something I call "guerilla mental healthcare." Trieste, Italy, is a pioneer in this, known for being one of the very first cities to deliberately shut down its psychiatric hospital and shift treatment to open-door clinics. From the hospital's void grew increased community acceptance, vocational training and work placements, housing support, and the restitution of human rights for the formerly locked away.</div> <br>
